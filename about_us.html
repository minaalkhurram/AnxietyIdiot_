The root cause of social anxiety

The root cause of all social anxiety attacks is fear. When we fear being judged by others, when we fear judgment for ourselves, when we don't fit into societal norms, or when we believe that it will be worse if we are judged — these are all triggers for our fears of embarrassment

Reason behind suddenly developing social anxiety

Social anxiety disorder can develop suddenly after a stressful or embarrassing experience, or slowly over time. There is some evidence that social anxiety runs in families, so there may be other members who share similar difficulties as your child.
